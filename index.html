R U B Y

Ruby. I'm not able to fully recall our first one on one interaction
but who remembers how relationships start? Ruby is the only person I
have ever opened up to, the only one I've told what goes on in my head,
the emotions that I have but sometimes cannot understand or feel. She
tolerates the fact that I'm different. She is different as well, and
special beyond her own comprehension. The way I feel at home with her
makes no sense at all. Ruby loves that I code. She appreciates what I
write. She makes me happy, makes me want to find and be a better version
of myself. She is sweet and considerate. Beautiful and smart. Grave and
naughty. She has magical eyes that cast a spell on the beholder and....
I could go on forever. I wrote this site as a tribute to her. Ruby, my best
friend, this is for you. I hope you like it. I love you.
--E.K
